Chronotype is the natural inclination of your body to sleep at a certain time. Or in simple words...being an early bird versus a night owl.
Lion
Early risers who feel most productive in the morning. They enjoy waking up with the sun!
Bear
Day-oriented individuals who follow the sun. They feel best during the day and sleep well at night.
Wolf
Night owls who thrive in the evening and find their creative energy after dark.
Dolphin
Light sleepers with erratic schedules. They perform best during quiet, focused times.
